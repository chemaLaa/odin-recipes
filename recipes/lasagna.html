Lasagna

Description

Making a classic, comforting lasagna involves simmering a rich meat sauce, mixing a creamy cheese filling, layering it with pasta, and baking it until bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Meat sauce
  • Cheese mixture
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Prepare the cheese layer
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. Bake and rest
